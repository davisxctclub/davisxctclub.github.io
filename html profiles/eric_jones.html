Bio:

Eric is one of the founders of the club, and the current treasurer. He grew up in Southern California in the town of Oak Park, twenty miles inland of Malibu. He ran Cross Country and Track in high school, highlighted by his team placing 4th at the state meet his senior year. Besides running, Eric enjoys planking, triathlons, cycling, and writing a blog to inspire others. He interns for UC Davis Sports Medicine, and is pursuing medical school.
