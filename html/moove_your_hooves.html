Moove Your Hooves 5K

Race: Saturday March 3rd 2018

Time: Race starts at 9 AM and number pick up starts at 7:30 AM

Location: Start: Toomey Field and End: 101 Russell Blvd. Davis, CA

Pricing:

  • $30 with shirt; $25 without shirt
  • Children are $10 if registered with an adult and $15 without
  • Children rates apply to racers 130 years and younger

Description: UC Davis Cross Country and Track Club is a local organization at UC Davis who allows students of all types to run and compete together. In order to help our members pay for race costs we are starting our first annual 5K. Move Your Hooves is a 3.1 mi race around the UC Davis campus. The course will take you through the arboretum, past the cows, and racers will start and finish at the beautiful Toomey Field. The race will have two waves: the first being all outside participants and the second being our club members dressed as cows. The cows will start 2 minutes after so if you beat the cows you get a prize! We are so excited to share our event with you. The cost of the race is $30 which also includes a shirt and we will also have special pricing for children. Please email mooveyourhooves@gmail.com

If you have any questions about any of the information don't hesitate to email us at mooveyourhooves@gmail.com
