UC Davis Varsity Team

Description

The Varsity Team is a group of athletes who train to compete and represent the UC Davis Cross Country and Track Club on the intercollegiate stage. These are self-selected individuals with a desire to train and compete at a high level. Under the direction of coaches Clancy McConnell and Nathan Kwan, the main goal is to bring the NIRCA National title back to UC Davis.
