Race Schedule Descriptions

# Race Name Date About
1 Stanford Invitational 9/26/15 This is a meet that requires a qualifying standard. It is not a team race, but rather determined on an individual basis. The course is on a golf course, and is flat.
2 San Francisco State Invitational 10/9/15 This is our first team race of the season. It is hosted by San Francisco State University, at beautiful Golden Gate Park. There are a few small hills, and great competition for our club here.
3 Bronco Invitational 10/17/15 Hosted by Santa Clara University, this flat course runs through Baylands Regional Park in Sunnyvale. Again, there is good competition at this race, and a definite chance for some fast times.
4 NIRCA Pacific Region Championship 10/24/15 This is our first race of the season against strictly club teams. We will face clubs from Oregon, Cal Poly SLO, UCLA, and more. The meet will be held at the Crystal Springs Cross Country Course, in Belmont. Stanfordâ€™s running club is hosting the meet, just like last year. This is a fairly hilly course, all on dirt.
5 Doc Adams Invitational (tentative) 11/7/15 This is the cross country meet held by UC Davis every year. It winds through the Putah Creek Reserve, only about a five minute drive from the UC Davis campus. It is basically all flat, as one would expect in Davis, so fast times are within reach.
6 NIRCA National Championship 11/14/15 This is the big one! All of the running clubs across America come together in Lexington, Kentucky on this day, to duke it out for the title of National Champion. The course looks to be all flat green grass, with surrounding farmland, just like Davis.
