Welcome to The UC Davis Cross Country & Track Club

We welcome runners of all levels of experience from those training for their first race up to national champions. Racing opportunities are available for Cross Country in the fall and Track & Field in the spring. Our social events and fundraisers benefit the club and allow us to accept runners year-round. Practices are held Monday through Friday at 4PM on the Woody Wilson Track and Toomey Field on the corner of A and 1st St. All are welcome to come out and enjoy running in a close-knit community!

To learn more about our club, visit our About Page!
